What is a chatbot?

Chatbot is a conversation system that uses Artificial Intelligence to generate interactions with humans. It has steadily been improved to generate natural dialogues that meet the demands of companies in a reliable and effective way.

What is a virtual assistant?

As a way of achieving customisation, several companies create an identity for bots that interact with users. This way, your chatbot can work with this personification, assuming the identity of a character. Your team now has a virtual colleague.

What can I do with the chatbot / virtual assistant?

With a chatbot / virtual assistant, you can ensure interactions with people who contact your company and need answers to questions instantly. This way, you don't have to keep an employee or be available 24 hours a day to answer questions about opening hours, orders, reservations, issuing invoices, among other functions.

Where can I use a chatbot?

You can use a chatbot on your company's website and in messaging applications such as Facebook Messenger.
